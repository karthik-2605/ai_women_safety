Welcome, Team Defence

Who Are We?

SafeHer is a women’s safety initiative driven by AI and voice distress detection. Our goal is to build a tech-enabled ecosystem that allows women to seek help swiftly and discreetly in times of distress.

We believe in creating impactful tools that empower women, provide real-time location tracking, emergency alerts, and a voice-activated beacon system.

What We Do

Voice Distress Detection

AI-powered model listens for distress keywords or tone and triggers immediate emergency actions.

Real-Time Location Sharing

Instantly shares the victim’s live location with guardians or emergency services.

Emergency Beacon

One-tap or voice-activated beacon sends alerts, location, and audio to pre-set contacts.

Safe Check-ins

Allows users to mark themselves safe or request help discreetly at regular intervals.

Secure Data Logs

Encrypted logs of events and locations to assist authorities with case follow-up.

Offline Mode

Even without internet, the app records voice commands and triggers local alerts.
